## 1 · Video · hero film · 15 sec

**"Morning ritual."**

```
Generate a 15 second cinematic film in the visual language of Oura Ring 4
and Halo Dental launch films. Warm cream and soft ink color grade. No
electric blues. No neon.

SHOT 1 (0-3s): Extreme close up of a woman in her 30s, eyes closed,
morning light raking across her cheek. A faint smile. Linen bedding in
soft focus behind her. Real, unretouched skin texture.

SHOT 2 (3-7s): She sits up on the edge of her bed. Her hand reaches to
a nightstand, picks up VertiBand. Shot from slightly behind her
shoulder. The band is the exact device in the reference image: soft
matte headband, small printed housing, warm neutral color. She turns
it over gently in her hands.

SHOT 3 (7-11s): She places it on her head. Camera holds on a three
quarter profile. Natural window light. No harsh shadows. She closes
her eyes and tilts her head 45 degrees to the right in a slow,
intentional movement. We see the quiet focus on her face.

SHOT 4 (11-15s): Pull back to a wide of her silhouetted against the
window, VertiBand on, perfectly still. Soft dust motes float in the
light shaft. Fade to a matte cream title card: "VertiBand" in a light
serif, italic wordmark, small.

Style: Kodak Portra 400 color science. 24fps. Shallow depth of field,
anamorphic 1.44x feel. Ambient bedroom sound only, no music. No text
overlays during the shots. Device in every frame must match the
reference photo exactly in shape and proportions.

Reference image: photo-headband-hero.png
```
